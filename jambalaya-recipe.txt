Ingredients:
 - 1 tbsp olive oil
 - 16 ozs kielbasa (cut into 1‚ÅÑ4-inch rounds)
 - 28 ozs tomatoes (whole, undrained)
 - 8 ozs jambalaya (Spanish, or fiesta-flavored rice mix)
 - 1 lb shrimp (peeled and deveined raw)
 - 1/4 tsp hot pepper sauce (optional)
 
Directions:
 1. Heat the oil in a saucepan over medium heat. 
 
 2. Add the kielbasa and cook until browned on both sides, about 4 minutes. 

 3. Add the tomatoes and their juices along with 1/2 cup of water and bring to 
    a boil. 

 4. Stir in the rice mix. Reduce heat, cover, and cook for the time specified 
    on the rice package. 

 5. Add the shrimp and stir. 

 6. Cover and cook until the shrimp are pink, 3 to 4 minutes. 

 7. Add the hot pepper sauce (if using). 

 8. Spoon into individual bowls.
